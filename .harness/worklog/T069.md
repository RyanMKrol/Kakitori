**What I did**

On iPhone (compact size class), `HomeView`'s `.sheet` presented `DeckSetupSheet` with
`.presentationDetents([.medium])`, which is too short to fit the sheet's content (header band,
practice-mode caption, four mode rows, pinned full-width "Start writing" CTA). The header ended up
detached/clipped and the CTA was reduced to a sliver at the bottom of the screen.

Fixed by changing `Sources/Features/Home/HomeView.swift` to always present `DeckSetupSheet` with
`.presentationDetents([.large])`, regardless of `horizontalSizeClass`. This is the simplest of the
options the task spec allowed ("use .large on compact too") and keeps `DeckSetupSheet`'s internal
layout (headerBand / mode list / pinned startButton) untouched. iPad's presentation was already
`.large`, so iPad behaviour is unchanged.
