DailyStats` with `day: String`, `cardsWritten: Int`, `newIntroduced: Int`, `reviewsDone: Int`,
`secondsStudied: Int`, and the matching defaulted `init`. Added the pure static
`DailyStats.currentStreak(activeDays:now:clock:)` implementing the display rule from
docs/02-product-spec.md §4.3 / docs/03-srs-algorithm.md §7: it walks backwards one calendar day at
a time via `clock.calendar.date(byAdding: .day, value: -1, to:)`, checking `clock.adjustedDay(for:)`

yesterday instead of failing immediately — so the streak isn't shown as broken until a full day has
actually passed. No SwiftData access inside the function; no force-unwraps.
